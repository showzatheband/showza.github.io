Showza is a band from Surrey, England. They play rambunctious stadium-punk but with pretty melodies. Some say they have taken influence from the likes of RHCP, with a bit of The Cure and a sprinkling of Primus. Although, we cannot truly be sure. The members are odd creatures who refuse to be defined, thus they are as their name suggests, Showza.


Showza are:

Alfred Morris (Vocals & Guitar)

James Down (Backing Vocals & Bass)

Max Collings (Drums)

Gillan Westley (Guitar)
